Our Story

ZenFlow Nepal was founded in 2021 in the heart of Thamel, Kathmandu. Surrounded by the spiritual energy of the Himalayas and rooted in Nepal's deep yogic traditions, our studio was created to help individuals reconnect with balance, mindfulness, and inner peace.

We blend authentic Himalayan yoga philosophy with modern wellness techniques to create accessible programs for students, professionals, travelers, and spiritual seekers. Our journey has been driven by a simple belief: that yoga is not just an exercise, but a transformative practice that connects the mind, body, and spirit.

Our Mission

To make authentic Himalayan yoga and meditation accessible to everyone, creating a sanctuary where individuals can discover their inner peace and unlock their full potential.
