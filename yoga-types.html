Our Yoga Styles

Discover the perfect yoga practice that resonates with your body, mind, and spirit

Find Your Perfect Practice

At ZenFlow, we offer a diverse range of yoga styles tailored to meet practitioners at every level. Whether you're seeking an energetic flow, a restorative practice, or deep meditation, we have a class designed for you.

Hatha Yoga

Traditional foundational yoga practiced for centuries in Himalayan regions. This gentle, beginner-friendly style focuses on basic postures held for longer periods, allowing practitioners to build strength, flexibility, and body awareness at a comfortable pace.

Perfect For:

Beginners, those seeking steady progress, and anyone building a solid foundation

View Classes

Vinyasa Flow

Dynamic, flowing practice that synchronizes breath with movement. Inspired by mountain energy and breath rhythm, this moving meditation creates a seamless flow. Classes build cardiovascular endurance, strength, and stamina while connecting to Himalayan energy.

Perfect For:

Intermediate practitioners, fitness enthusiasts, and those seeking energetic flow

View Classes

Yin Yoga

A slower-paced practice where poses are held for 3-5 minutes or longer. Ideal for cold Himalayan mornings, Yin yoga targets deep connective tissues and promotes relaxation, flexibility, and meditation. This restorative style is perfect for balance and recovery.

Perfect For:

Stress relief, deep stretching, recovery days, and gaining inner peace

View Classes

Himalayan Traditional Yoga

Rooted in Nepal's ancient yogic heritage and Buddhist meditation practices. This disciplined practice follows traditional sequences passed down through generations in the Himalayan regions.

Perfect For:

Those seeking authentic traditional practices and spiritual growth through proven lineages

View Classes

Sunrise Himalayan Flow

Experience outdoor rooftop yoga sessions with views of the Kathmandu valley and surrounding Himalayan mountains. Feel the mountain energy as you flow with the rising sun in Thamel.

Perfect For:

Those seeking energetic flow with Himalayan mountain energy and breathtaking natural surroundings

View Classes

Meditation Sessions

Deepen your mind-body connection through guided meditation inspired by Buddhist practices at Boudhanath and Lumbini. Whether focusing on breath, visualization, or mantra, meditation cultivates mental clarity, emotional balance, and spiritual awakening in the sacred Kathmandu valley.

Perfect For:

Stress reduction, mental clarity, emotional balance, and spiritual transformation

View Classes

Kundalini Yoga

Experience the awakening of energy through this transformative practice combining breathwork, chanting, and dynamic movements. Kundalini yoga activates your inner power, enhances intuition, and creates profound spiritual transformation. Perfect for those seeking deeper energetic healing with Himalayan wisdom traditions.

Perfect For:

Energy activation, spiritual seekers, and those wanting transformative inner awakening experiences

View Classes

Restorative Yoga

A gentle, supported practice using props to help your body recover and rejuvenate. Poses are held for extended periods in complete comfort, allowing deep nervous system relaxation. Ideal for healing, recovery, and those managing injuries or chronic conditions with compassionate self-care.

Perfect For:

Recovery, injury rehabilitation, stress management, and improving overall wellbeing
