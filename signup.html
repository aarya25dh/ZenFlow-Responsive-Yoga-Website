Sign-Up Questions?

Is there a trial period or free class? +

Yes! New members get one completely free trial class. No credit card needed. You can experience our studio, meet our instructors, and find a class that resonates with you before committing.

Do I need to pay anything upfront? +

No payment is required until you decide to join. Your first class is completely free. When you're ready to purchase a membership, enter your payment information at checkout.

Can I change my membership plan later? +

Absolutely! You can upgrade to a higher tier or downgrade anytime. Contact our front desk or manage your account in our mobile app. Changes take effect on your next billing cycle.

What if I need to cancel? +

You can cancel anytime with no penalties. Monthly memberships require 14-day notice. Annual memberships offer pro-rata refunds within 90 days. We'll be sad to see you go, but we understand life happens!

How do I start attending classes? +

After creating your account, you'll receive confirmation and a welcome email with next steps. Book your first free trial class through our website or call us at (555) 123-YOGA. Arrive 10-15 minutes early and we'll help you get set up!
